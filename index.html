Finger Zone Map

The keyboard is divided into three vertical zones. The outer-left columns are operated by the thumb, the wide centre band by the three middle fingers (index, middle, ring), and the outer-right columns by the pinky. This fused version keeps that section styling and legend, but swaps the rough hex mockup for the exact SVG reconstruction of your mapped keyboard.

Hover a key to inspect its assigned zone.
Thumb — outer left columns
3 Middle Fingers — centre zone
Pinky — outer right columns
