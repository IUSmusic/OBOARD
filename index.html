How to use

  1. Start the camera. Halo will profile your device and switch to fast mode automatically if needed.
  2. Calibrate by holding your hand flat, or relaxed if you are using Bed mode, then tap the calibration button.
  3. Use One-hand sculpt to place the keyboard. Pinch to move it and change wrist angle to rotate it.
  4. Pin the keyboard and type with press, pinch, dwell, voice, or the touch mirror if the camera becomes unreliable.
  5. In pointer mode, choose index, any finger, or whole hand for the cursor. Air pinch clicks, pinch-hold drags, two-finger motion scrolls, and two-hand pinch zoom adjusts the page scale.

Notes

Shift and Caps can be toggled from the keyboard, by voice, or with a two-finger swipe gesture. Browser PWAs can drive on-page navigation and controls, but true system-wide phone control would require an accessibility-service style wrapper outside this web app.

First run

Welcome to Halo

  1. Pinch to place the keyboard.
  2. Poke to type, or switch to pointer mode.
  3. Use the mirror overlay for at-a-glance confidence.
  4. Bed mode tunes smoothing and thresholds for relaxed single-hand use.
Calibration

Hold your hand flat

After the camera starts, hold your hand flat above the surface and tap capture. Halo will measure span, tilt, and depth so press thresholds and keyboard size feel more natural.
